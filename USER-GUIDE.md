# User Guide

A complete walkthrough of the Hypnosis Script Generator — from first visit to your first session.

---

## Getting Started

Open the generator on Perchance.org. Everything runs in your browser — no account, no download, no setup. Your data stays on your device.

The tool is a 5-step wizard. Each step builds on the last. Defaults are sensible — you can click through to Step 5 and generate a session in under a minute, or customise everything.

---

## Step 1: Choose Your Guide

The **guide** is the voice personality that narrates your session. Each guide has a distinct tone, vocabulary, and approach.

### Categories

**Contemplative** — Gentle, introspective, unhurried. Best for first-time users and therapeutic work.
- *The Driver* — sparse, road-and-engine metaphors, long silences.
- *The Therapist* — warm, professional, permissive language ("allowing", "noticing").
- *The Scientist* — demystifying, explains mechanisms as they happen.
- *The Mystic* — archetypal, thresholds and still waters.

**Showman** — Theatrical, confident, entertaining. Good for demonstrations and novelty effects.
- *The Showman* — direct stage-hypnotist authority.
- *The Mentalist* — mysterious, implies mind-reading.
- *The Carnival Barker* — rhythmic, builds anticipation.

**Somatic** — Body-focused, works through physical sensation. Excellent for pain management and body awareness.
- *The Bodyworker* — tissue-level awareness, fascia and warmth.
- *The Yoga Teacher* — breath-body connection, grounded.
- *The Martial Artist* — centre, root, iron stillness.

**Authoritarian** — Commanding, intense, direct. For experienced users who respond to structure and authority.
- *The Commander* — military precision, pure directive.
- *The Headmistress* — Victorian poise, structured discipline.
- *The Dominatrix* — controlled power exchange, cold precision.

### Custom Personas
Click **+ create custom** to design your own guide. Give it a name, tagline, category, and a voice instruction that tells the AI how to write in that voice.

### Suggestibility Quiz
Expand **"hypnotizability self-assessment"** for a quick 3-question quiz that suggests which methods and guides might work best for you.

### Saved Scripts & History
Expand **"saved scripts"** to reload a previous session, or **"session history & stats"** to see your streak, total minutes, and favourite guide.

## Step 2: Choose Your Method

The **method** is *how* trance will be induced — the specific technique the AI will use.

### Categories

**Therapeutic** — Slower, subtler, building trance through relaxation or distraction. Start here.
- *Highway* — sensory monotony with scrolling road visuals. Best for analytical minds.
- *4-7-8 Breath* — pure physiology. Parasympathetic activation.
- *Progressive Relaxation* — head-to-toe body scan. Classic.
- *Betty Erickson 3-2-1* — structured sensory countdown.
- *Staircase Descent* — imaginative descent, each step deeper.

**Somatic** — Work through the body. Physical sensations are the mechanism.
- *Body Awareness Scan* — micro-sensation focus. Depth of attention IS the induction.
- *Glove Anesthesia* — hand goes numb. Transfer numbness anywhere.
- *Heartbeat Synchronisation* — slow your own heart rate.

**Demonstration** — Produce undeniable physical or cognitive effects you can test.
- *Magnetic Hands* — fingers draw together on their own.
- *Arm Catalepsy* — arm extends and locks rigid.
- *Number Amnesia* — forget the number 7.
- *Stuck to Your Seat* — cannot stand up.

**Rapid** — Fast inductions for experienced users. Seconds, not minutes.
- *Dave Elman* — the clinical standard. 1-3 minutes.
- *Shock Induction* — "SLEEP!" Instant authority drop.
- *Arm Drop* — arm up, arm drops, you drop with it.

**Advanced** — Deep trance phenomena. Use after you're comfortable with basic trance.
- *Time Distortion* — 5 minutes feels like an hour.
- *Positive Hallucination* — see something that isn't there.
- *Double Induction* — two voices simultaneously.
- *Eyes-Open Somnambulism* — deepest trance, eyes open.

## Step 3: Set Your Intention

Choose what you want from the session. Select a **type of change** from the dropdown, then **describe what you want** in plain language.

Click **"craft hypnotic suggestion →"** to have the AI convert your rough description into a technically-precise hypnotic suggestion. This takes 1–3 minutes.

You can **edit** the crafted suggestion manually or **re-craft** it.

Some intention types (like *"Just experience trance"* and *"Sleep induction"*) don't need a crafted suggestion — the craft controls hide automatically.

### Intention Types

**Foundation** — emotional shifts, triggers, behaviour change, anchors.
**Performance** — peak flow, deep focus, creative unblocking.
**Body** — pain management, sleep, energy boost.
**Demonstration** — convincers and party tricks.
**Advanced** — time distortion, amnesia, identity play, surrender, conditioning.

## Step 4: Configure the Session

### Session Length
- **Micro** (~2 min) — quick relief booster.
- **Quick** (~5 min) — focused reset.
- **Short** (~12 min) — standard session.
- **Medium** (~20 min) — recommended for most uses.
- **Long** (~30 min) — deepest immersion.

### Voice
Select from available TTS voices. Voices are sorted by quality — ★ indicates neural/natural voices, ● indicates high-quality cloud voices.

Click **▶ preview voice** to hear a sample.

### Audio & Soundscape
Choose an ambient soundscape to play alongside narration. All sounds are procedurally generated — they never loop.

- **None** — voice only.
- **Engine drone** — low hum. Recommended for Highway method.
- **Rain** — soft droplets and rumble.
- **Ocean waves** — rhythmic surf on an 11-second cycle.
- **Crackling fire** — warm pops and brown noise.
- **Singing bowls** — periodic resonant strikes.
- **Heartbeat** — steady lub-dub.
- **Binaural beats** — theta 6Hz. **Requires stereo headphones.**
- **Pink / Brown / White noise** — three noise flavours.

Adjust **soundscape volume**, **voice speed**, **voice pitch**, and **narration volume** with sliders.

### Visual Effect
Choose from 16 visual effects, or leave on **Auto** to use the method's recommended visual. Effects are grouped by intensity: Induction, Deepening, Trance, Intense, Minimal.

### Display Elements
Toggle individual session HUD elements on or off: fixation dot, narration text, phase dots, phase label, progress bar, elapsed time, corner labels.

---

## Step 5: Generate & Begin

Review your choices in the summary cards. The **brief preview** shows exactly what the AI will be asked to generate.

Click **"generate full script"** — this makes one AI call and takes 20–40 seconds. A progress indicator shows elapsed time.

Once generated, you can:
- **Read and edit** the full script in the text area.
- **Regenerate** for a different version.
- **Save to library** for future use.
- **Download as .txt** for offline reading.
- **Share config** — copies a link that pre-fills the wizard for someone else.

### Pre-Session Mood Check
Optionally tap an emoji (😔 😐 🙂 😊 ✨) to record how you feel before the session. This is saved alongside the session record for tracking mood deltas over time.

Click **"begin session →"** to start.

## During the Session

### What You'll See
- The selected **visual effect** fills the stage area.
- **Phase dots** at the top show which phase you're in.
- **Narration text** displays each sentence as it's spoken.
- A **progress bar** tracks overall progress.
- **Elapsed time** shows how long you've been in session.

### Controls
| Control | Action |
|---|---|
| **pause / resume** | Pause or resume narration and soundscape |
| **end & re-alert** | Emergency stop — speaks a persona-matched re-alert script |
| **📜 script** | Toggle the full transcript overlay |
| **⏺ record ambience** | Start/stop recording the soundscape as a .webm file |
| **⛶ fullscreen** | Enter/exit fullscreen on the stage |
| **⚙ settings** | Open the live settings panel |
| **Space** (keyboard) | Pause / resume |
| **Escape** (keyboard) | Emergency end |

### Live Settings Panel
Click **⚙ settings** to open a slide-out panel where you can adjust **everything** mid-session without stopping:
- Switch the visual effect
- Change the soundscape type (stops and restarts)
- Adjust soundscape volume, voice speed, pitch, and volume
- Toggle any HUD element on or off

Changes apply immediately.

---

## After the Session

### Normal Completion
You'll see a gentle landing screen with a **session summary** (guide, method, duration, completion status). If you set a trigger or suggestion, it's displayed as a reminder card. Demonstration methods show a **"test now"** prompt.

### Emergency End
If you end early, you'll see the **5-4-3-2-1 grounding exercise** automatically. There's a 30-second cooldown before you can start a new session.

### Mood Check-In
Tap an emoji to record how you feel after the session. This is saved to your session history.

### Save & Start Again
**Save to library** saves the script for re-use. **Begin a new session** reloads the wizard.

---

## Data Management

### Backup & Restore
Expand **"backup & restore"** on Step 1 to:
- **Export backup** — downloads all custom personas, saved scripts, and session history as a single JSON file.
- **Import backup** — merges data from a backup file (keeps existing, adds new, no duplicates).

### Session History
Expand **"session history & stats"** to see total sessions, total minutes meditated, current day streak, completion rate, and your most-used guide. The last 10 sessions are listed with dates and durations.

## Tips

- **First session?** Use The Therapist + Progressive Relaxation + medium length. Keep the defaults.
- **Analytical mind?** Try The Scientist + Betty Erickson 3-2-1 or Highway.
- **Want proof it works?** Try The Showman + Magnetic Hands. The fingers move on their own.
- **Sleep?** Use any contemplative guide + Progressive Relaxation + Sleep intention + Ocean or Rain soundscape + Long length.
- **Headphones** make a significant difference for binaural beats and spatial immersion.
- **Don't drive** immediately after a deep session. Give yourself a few minutes.
- **The first 5–8 minutes feel like nothing** with therapeutic methods — that's the mechanism. Don't give up.
